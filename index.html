Hello, I'm Musa Jalili

A dedicated Ph.D. Scholar at Hazara University, Pakistan, specializing in the dynamic fields of Remote Sensing, Image Processing, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. My academic journey commenced with a Bachelor's degree from Kabul Education University, a pivotal experience that laid the groundwork for my prowess in computer programming. Continuing my pursuit of knowledge, I pursued and successfully earned a Master's degree from South Asian University, India, further refining my research skills and expanding my expertise. In tandem with my academic pursuits, I have had the honor of serving as an Assistant Professor at both [Rana University] and [Hewad University]. In these roles, I passionately imparted knowledge in subjects such as Digital Logic Design, Software Engineering, and Software Project Management, fostering meaningful engagement with my students. My research is firmly rooted in the realm of Deep Learning techniques, with a focused ambition to drive advancements in fully remote sensing for agricultural applications. Through my work, I aspire to contribute significantly to the ongoing evolution of this critical field. Thank you for taking the time to visit my personal website and learn more about my journey and aspirations.

Feel free to contact me at jalilimusa@gmail.com to discuss collaboration, and opportunities, or just to connect!
